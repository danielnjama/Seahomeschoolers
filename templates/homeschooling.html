Hello, From Secular Eclectic Academic Homeschoolers!

Blair Lee M.S

Founder of SEA Homeschoolers & Author, TEACHER SEA ONLINE CLASSES

Blair Lee earned Bachelor degrees in Biology and Chemistry and a Master’s degree in Chemistry at the University of California San Diego. Blair is the author of 14 books including The Science of Climate Change: A Hands-On Course and Project-Based Learning: Creating a Modern Education of Curiosity, Innovation, and Impact which she co-authored with Samantha Cook. Blair is the primary author for the critically acclaimed REAL Science Odyssey Series, that The Well-Trained Mind has rated four years in a row as one of the top three science programs for first through eighth grades. She has been involved in science education for over two decades, first as a college professor and secondly as a curriculum developer. Over the past six years, she has broadened her scope and done extensive consulting as well as multi-discipline program development for written course materials and more recently for live and taped online classes. Blair writes concept-rich, hands-on courses. She is a passionate advocate of innovative academics where the focus is on how subjects are best learned. Through her speaking, writing, and consulting, her goal is to empower educators to dare to be innovative and create something unique and academically-rich when handcrafting their students’ education. Blair is the founder of Secular, Eclectic, Academic (SEA) Homeschoolers and SEA Books & More.
